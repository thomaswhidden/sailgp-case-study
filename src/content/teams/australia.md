## Ownership History

Australia was one of the six original league-owned teams at SailGP's 2019 inaugural season. Tom Slingsby has been the skipper since founding and led the team to three consecutive championships in Seasons 1, 2, and 3. The team became the league's dominant dynasty in its early years.

Ryan Reynolds and Hugh Jackman became controlling owners in June 2025. The acquisition was one of the most publicized transactions in the league's history, generating global media coverage across entertainment, sports, and business press. The BONDS clothing brand (Australian underwear/apparel) serves as the title sponsor, giving the team its full name: "BONDS Flying Roos."

## Valuation

Reported valuation of $60M+ as of 2025. The team's three championships, strong fan base in Australia (a core SailGP market), and celebrity ownership premium all support this valuation relative to peers.

## Competitive Record

- Season 1 (2019): Champions
- Season 2 (2020–21): Champions
- Season 3 (2021–22): Champions
- Seasons 4–5: Competitive but not champions; strong event wins

## Strategic Notes

The Reynolds/Jackman ownership brings exceptional earned media reach. Reynolds alone has 50M+ Instagram followers; Jackman is globally recognized across demographics that SailGP has struggled to reach. The team is the clearest example of the celebrity ownership strategy delivering media value.
