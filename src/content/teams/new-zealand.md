## Overview

New Zealand (the Black Foils) won the Season 4 championship in 2024 under Peter Burling's leadership. Burling is one of the most decorated sailors in the world: multiple Olympic medals, America's Cup winner with Emirates Team New Zealand.

The team is one of the last remaining league-owned teams in SailGP (alongside possibly one or two others being transitioned in 2026). The league's strategy is to move all teams to private ownership, and NZ is in an active sale process as of early 2026.

## Racing Incident — February 2026

New Zealand was involved in the NZ–France collision in Auckland during Season 6 (February 2026). Both teams were sidelined. The collision occurred in the harbor during an event — a significant safety incident at a high-speed racing event in a crowded waterway.

## Strategic Notes

New Zealand is a high-prestige team given the country's sailing culture and Peter Burling's global profile. A private buyer at or above the $60M+ benchmark would validate league-wide valuations. The identity of the eventual buyer will be an important data point for the case.

The collision creates a complication for the sale process — buyers will want to understand the operational and reputational consequences before committing.
