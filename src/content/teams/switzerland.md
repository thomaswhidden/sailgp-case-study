## Ownership

Ernesto Bertarelli leads the Swiss team's ownership. Bertarelli is a notable figure in sailing history:
- His **Alinghi** syndicate won the America's Cup in 2003 and 2007, the second and third consecutive wins by a non-US team in the Cup's modern era.
- He is a billionaire biotech entrepreneur (Serono pharmaceuticals, sold to Merck KGaA).
- He has been involved in competitive sailing at the highest levels for decades.

Bertarelli's entry into SailGP is significant: he is one of the few team owners with direct experience at the America's Cup level, which makes his choice to invest in SailGP rather than (or in addition to) a Cup challenge a statement about SailGP's commercial trajectory.

## Strategic Notes

Switzerland joined in 2024 as an expansion team. The Swiss domestic market is affluent and sports-interested (Formula 1 fan base, ski racing), but Switzerland is landlocked, which creates an interesting brand narrative for a sailing team.

The Bertarelli connection to both the America's Cup world and SailGP is potentially significant for the "America's Cup relationship" open question — Bertarelli has competed against Coutts at the Cup level and now invests alongside him at SailGP.
