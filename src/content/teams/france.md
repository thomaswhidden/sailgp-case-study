## Ownership and Investment History

France SailGP has assembled one of the most commercially interesting cap tables in the league. Kylian Mbappé — at the time the world's most commercially valuable footballer — joined as an investor prior to the Ares Management deal. Ares Management (AUM: ~$450B) took a minority stake in June 2025, bringing institutional PE credibility.

This is one of the clearest examples of SailGP attracting two very different investor profiles: global celebrity athlete (Mbappé) and large-scale institutional manager (Ares). The combination is notable because Ares brings governance discipline that celebrity owners typically do not.

## Racing Incident — February 2026

The France team was involved in the NZ–France collision in Auckland (February 2026). Both teams were sidelined. The cause and consequences of the incident, and any operational changes resulting from it, are an open question for the case study.

## Strategic Notes

France's domestic market (sailing culture, large population, TV market) makes it one of the higher-potential teams commercially. Quentin Delapierre has been one of the more competitive drivers in recent seasons.

The Ares stake is significant for the "investor quality" criterion — it suggests PE sees SailGP team ownership as a return-generating investment, not just a marketing expense.
