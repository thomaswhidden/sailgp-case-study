## Ownership History

The Denmark team was formerly named ROCKWOOL Racing (ROCKWOOL is a Danish building materials company). In February 2026, the team was acquired by Doug DeVos and American Magic.

**Doug DeVos** is co-owner of the Orlando Magic (NBA) and a member of the DeVos family (Amway founders). This is a sophisticated sports ownership group with existing infrastructure in professional sports management.

**American Magic** is the US Challenger for the America's Cup — the connection between SailGP and America's Cup ownership is notable. American Magic's acquisition of a SailGP team suggests that some America's Cup programs view SailGP ownership as complementary rather than competing.

## Valuation Significance

The $60M+ acquisition in February 2026 set a record for SailGP team valuation and provides the clearest current market pricing benchmark. The transaction followed the DeVos group apparently deciding that SailGP team ownership was a more accessible entry point than building another full America's Cup challenge.

## Competitive Notes

The team has been competitive but has not won the championship. The new ownership group may bring the management sophistication to improve both racing and commercial performance.
