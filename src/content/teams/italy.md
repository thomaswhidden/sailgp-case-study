## Ownership

Italy is one of the newer SailGP teams, joining in 2025. The ownership structure combines:

- **Jimmy Spithill** — one of the most accomplished America's Cup skippers of his generation (Oracle Team USA victories). He serves as CEO of the team entity and co-owner, bringing operational authority and sailing credibility to the ownership group.
- **Phil Robertson** — skipper/driver. Robertson was previously the "Mad Max of SailGP" persona at Canada; his move to Italy brings proven on-water aggression to a new team.
- **Anne Hathaway** — Academy Award-winning actress. Her involvement follows the celebrity ownership playbook established by Reynolds/Jackman (Australia) and Mbappé (France).
- **Red Bull** — as title sponsor, Red Bull brings significant marketing resources, a global distribution network, and the "extreme sport" brand association that aligns with SailGP's visual product.
- **Muse Capital** — the financial vehicle organizing the ownership structure.

## Valuation

At ~$45M (2025), Italy is valued below the $60M+ benchmark for more established teams. As a new entrant, the discount reflects the startup risk and lack of championship history. If the team performs well, the valuation gap to peers should narrow.

## Competitive Notes

Spithill's America's Cup pedigree makes Italy a credible sporting contender despite being a new team. His aggressive racing style and high profile in the sailing world make this team worth watching for competitive breakout potential.
