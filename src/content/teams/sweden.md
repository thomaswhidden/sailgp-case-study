## Ownership

**Torbjörn Törnqvist** is the principal owner. He is a Swedish billionaire who founded Gunvor Group, one of the world's largest commodity trading companies (oil, gas, metals). His wealth is estimated in the billions.

The Artemis Racing name carries significant sailing heritage: Artemis was the Swedish America's Cup challenger in the 2013 cycle, where a training accident tragically killed sailor Andrew Simpson. The brand was subsequently associated with strong technical performance in the 2017 Cup cycle under Iain Percy.

Reusing the Artemis brand for the SailGP team is a statement of continuity with Swedish competitive sailing heritage.

## Entry into Season 6

Sweden/Artemis joined as the 13th team for Season 6 (January 2026), bringing the league to its current roster. The expansion from 12 to 13 teams signals continued league growth, though it remains well below the stated 20-team cap.

## Strategic Notes

Törnqvist's background in energy commodities trading is interesting given SailGP's sustainability positioning. His investment diversification into sports follows a pattern common among commodity wealth holders (see: Ofer family, Bertarelli family). The valuation at the $60M+ level for a new entrant suggests the league's franchise pricing has converged to a standard tier.
