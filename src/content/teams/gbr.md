## Ownership

Ben Ainslie leads the ownership group as CEO. Ainslie is the most decorated Olympic sailor in history (4 gold medals, 1 silver) and a former America's Cup skipper with INEOS Team UK. Dylan Fletcher serves as the team's skipper/driver. Ainslie's role as owner-CEO rather than active driver is consistent with SailGP's sailor-led ownership model.

Emirates Airlines signed a 5-year global partnership with SailGP in November 2024, making Emirates the title sponsor of the GBR team — one of the highest-profile team-level sponsorships in the league.

## Competitive Record

- Seasons 1–4: Competitive, multiple race wins
- Season 5 (2024–25): Champions

Winning Season 5 — the most competitive season in the league's history with 12 teams and 12 events — is a significant sporting achievement for the team under Dylan Fletcher's helm.

## Strategic Notes

GBR is one of the most credible teams in the league from both a sporting and commercial perspective. Ainslie's profile is equivalent to a high-profile athlete-entrepreneur. The Emirates partnership is evidence that premium aviation brands see value in SailGP team-level sponsorship.

Portsmouth is typically one of the highest-attendance events on the SailGP calendar (20,000+ spectators), which reinforces the team's commercial case to sponsors.
