## Strengths

- +58 average Net Promoter Score across events in Season 5 — this is a high NPS for a sports property and suggests strong attendee satisfaction.
- 112,000+ ticketed spectators in Season 5. 25,000 in Auckland; 20,000 in Portsmouth. These are meaningful numbers.
- Season 5 total viewership across all broadcast channels was significant — the Abu Dhabi 3.47M US viewers figure was a record.
- The "Uncharted" docuseries (July 2025) is the key fan acquisition investment. Docuseries like Drive to Survive have transformed F1's casual audience; whether Uncharted achieves comparable impact is the key near-term question.
- Celebrity ownership (Reynolds, Jackman, Hathaway, Mbappé, Vettel) generates continuous earned media coverage in non-sailing outlets — the "celebrity halo" strategy is a legitimate alternative to traditional sports marketing.
- Social media amplification from celebrity owners is measurable and significant. Reynolds alone has 50M+ Instagram followers.

## Weaknesses / Risks

- Fan-team affinity is weak relative to traditional sports leagues. SailGP events are largely attended by local spectators (tourist model) rather than traveling team fans. There is no equivalent of a Manchester United road trip crowd, or even an F1 Tifosi.
- The demographic profile of SailGP spectators is unknown publicly. If it skews 45+, male, affluent coastal — the audience ceiling for both broadcast and sponsorship is limited.
- Social media following for SailGP teams is not publicly comparable to major sports franchises. The league's own social following is growing but from a low base.
- The racing format is still not self-explanatory to new fans. Unlike F1 (fastest car wins) or tennis (best of three sets), foiling sailing scoring requires explanation. This creates a content strategy burden.

## Open Questions

- What is the demographic profile of SailGP spectators and TV viewers? Age, gender, income, geography?
- What percentage of spectators at each event are local vs. traveling? Is there a traveling "SailGP fan" cohort forming?
- Has "Uncharted" measurably moved fan acquisition metrics — new social followers, streaming subscriptions, event ticket demand?
- What is the strategy for building deep team-fan affinity rather than event-tourist attendance?
- Is there a fan membership or club program in development?
