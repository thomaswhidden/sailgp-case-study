- **The franchise model has proven viable.** 13 teams in Season 6, all privately owned or in transition. Top team revenue at $8–9M; 3–4 teams break-even. Franchise valuations at $50M+ floor with $60M+ recent transactions.

- **The audience is real.** $200M+ league revenue. 3.47M US viewers for Race to Abu Dhabi. Auckland 70%-non-sailor audience composition. TikTok virality demonstrated.

- **The institutional layer has been built.** Global HQ in Southampton; cost cap; central services; transfer market; athlete equity model; multi-year sponsor commitments; Uncharted docuseries; Bet365 odds.

- **The next-phase question.** How to allocate capital and management attention across multiple credible priorities, without diluting coherence.
