# Realistic top-line revenue ceiling per team

## What's known

Thompson (Aug 2025): **top teams generate $8–9M USD per season**. Cost cap is $10M. Top revenue sources for teams: boat sponsorship space, hospitality, merchandise, in-territory event partnership, plus 50% of league broadcast distribution.

## What's not known

Where the ceiling is. F1 teams generate $300M+ at the top end. NFL franchises clear $500M. SailGP team revenue is currently 1/30th to 1/50th of those benchmarks. Coutts has framed SailGP as "the next Formula 1" — but that vision implies a 30-50x revenue scale-up at the team level.

## Questions to ask Coutts

1. What's the realistic top-line revenue ceiling for a SailGP team at maturity? $15M? $25M? $50M+?
2. Which revenue streams scale most aggressively — sponsorship, broadcast share, hospitality, or licensing?
3. At what team revenue level does SailGP justify a multi-billion-dollar league valuation?
4. What's the team revenue benchmark you'd need to hit for SailGP to be considered "the F1 of sailing" rather than a successful niche league?
