- What is the formal governance structure of SailGP? Is there a board of directors? An owners' council with voting rights? What decisions are reserved to the league vs. delegated to owners?
- How are owner conflicts resolved? Has there been a significant owner dispute that the public record doesn't capture? What is the mechanism?
- With celebrity owners (Reynolds, Jackman, Hathaway, Mbappé), how active vs. passive are they in practice? Do they attend races? Do they weigh in on sporting or commercial decisions?
- What happens if a major owner (say, Mubadala or Ares) wants to exit? Is there a right-of-first-refusal for the league or other owners? How liquid is the ownership stake in practice?
- Is there a formal process for approving new team owners? Who has veto rights?
