# Group A / Group B format mechanics

## What's known

Thompson (Aug 2025) confirmed SailGP is actively considering a format change to two pools of teams ("Group A and Group B") to support the move from 12 to 20 teams. The commercial rationale is sharp: currently 4 races run in a 1.5-hour broadcast window with ~10 minute gaps that cause measurable viewership drop-off. Two concurrent fleets allow back-to-back racing without breaks.

## Questions to ask Coutts

1. Is the Group A / Group B split a competitive division (top 10 vs. bottom 10, with promotion/relegation between them) or a logistical split (random assignment, both groups race for the same championship)?
2. If competitive: what's the promotion/relegation mechanism? Bottom 2 of A drop, top 2 of B promote? Or single-team swap?
3. Are there separate Group A and Group B champions, or one overall champion?
4. How does the season-long points system change to accommodate the split?
5. What's the implementation timeline — Season 7? Season 8?
6. Have you modeled commercial impact on team valuations of being in Group B vs. Group A — does it create a tier risk for franchise values?
7. Has the format change been discussed with broadcast partners? What's their reception?
