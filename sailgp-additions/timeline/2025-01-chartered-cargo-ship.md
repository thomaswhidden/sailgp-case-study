Per Andrew Thompson's August 2025 interview, SailGP's logistics operation handles transport between iconic global venues using a fleet of approximately 14 forty-foot containers. The F50 catamarans are designed with detachable bows ("like a Meccano set") so a 50-foot boat fits into a standard 40-foot shipping container, disassembled within 24 hours of a race.

Thompson cited shipping industry challenges as the rationale for chartering a dedicated ship, noting "stakes are a little bit higher than they once were." This is a notable operational risk-management decision — single-supplier shipping risk is one of the operational vulnerabilities a global league with synchronized event scheduling faces.

DP World is the long-term logistics partner. The centralization of logistics across all 12+ teams produces economies of scale that would be impossible if each team handled transport independently.
