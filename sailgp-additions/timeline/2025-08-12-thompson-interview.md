In a wide-ranging Business of Sport podcast interview, SailGP Managing Director Andrew Thompson made several previously-undisclosed operational and financial revelations:

- The current $10M team cost cap covers everything (salaries, travel, training, marketing, logistics) and is under active discussion to exclude marketing/social media spend
- 3-4 teams will hit at least break-even this season (2024-25)
- Top teams generate $8-9M in annual revenue
- Each F50 boat costs $5M to manufacture
- Franchise floor is now $50M, up from cost-covering investments at founding
- SailGP is considering a Group A / Group B format to enable growth to 20 teams while keeping broadcast windows tight
- Bet365 has already taken SailGP odds; betting infrastructure is in development
- 50% of league broadcast revenue is distributed to teams
